Miami, Florida


December 7th, 1993. The sun was in Sagittarius, the moon was in Virgo, and Capricorn was on the horizon line. At 8:43 in the morning, Randy and Cork Kallen welcomed their baby girl in an apartment building on Collins Avenue in Miami, Florida, overlooking the bay. After 36 hours of labor, Casey Emmis Kallen came into the world, ready to raise hell.
